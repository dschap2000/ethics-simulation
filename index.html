The Mission

Madison, Wisconsin.

Two organizations are dedicated to serving hungry people in the community. Amidst hostile immigration crackdowns, the mission is urgent: serve as many undocumented and fearful families as possible to ensure no one goes hungry in the shadows.

But how they achieve this goal will define their legacy.

YEAR TWO: THE SHORTCUT

Faced with intense pressure to hit internal targets, an Apex Metro Relief employee commits a severe ethical violation.

They secretly breach the local school district's database, stealing the highly sensitive addresses of undocumented families to bypass the slow work of community outreach.
