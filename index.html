The Mission

Madison, Wisconsin.

Forward Community Pantry and Apex Metro Relief are both dedicated to feeding the hungry. Amidst hostile immigration crackdowns, their mission is urgent: serve as many undocumented and fearful families as possible to ensure no one goes hungry in the shadows.

But how they achieve this goal will define their legacy.

YEAR TWO: THE SHORTCUT

Faced with intense pressure to hit internal targets, an Apex Metro Relief employee commits a severe ethical violation.

They secretly breach the local school district's database, stealing the highly sensitive addresses of undocumented families to bypass the slow work of community outreach.

SYSTEM FAILURE

A routine IT migration at the school district has uncovered the unauthorized data query from 5 years ago!

State and federal regulators have descended on Apex Metro Relief. Their funding is immediately frozen. The community is terrified, and the resulting data-lockdown paralyzes the city's social services.
